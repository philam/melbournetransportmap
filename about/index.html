Melbourne needs a transport map

PTV currently publish local area maps that show public transport in locations across Victoria. However, these are only useful for certain purposes. There is also no single map showing all public transport across Melbourne.

This project aims to create a new map showing all public transport available in metropolitan Melbourne that is user-friendly, visually-efficient and comprehensive.

Making this map is a huge undertaking and I need your help! I am looking for feedback on what you need from a public transport map and would greatly appreciate anything you could tell me.

To fill out the survey, please visit this page.

I hope to have the project completed by mid-2019. You can stay up to date on its progress on Twitter, Facebook or email.
